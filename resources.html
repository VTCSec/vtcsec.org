Useful Resources

Here are a list of useful resources for some hands-on learning.

HackTheBox - href these links

Hack your way into this site and discover hundreds of machines susceptible to one vulnerability or another. Gain a foothold on each machine and escalate your privileges to root or administrator. HackTheBox also hosts CTF-like challenges so that you can further hone your skills. Additionally, check out HackTheBox Academy for guided learning. 

TryHackMe

TryHackMe is a great way for beginners as it offers a lot of guided learning material for free. Walkthrough many different rooms on TryHackMe, attempt to clear each one, and grow your knowledge.  

OverTheWire

OverTheWire is an excellent resource for learning the fundamentals of security principles related to Linux, web-security, and other wargames.
