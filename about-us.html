About 

What is CyberVT?


CyberVT is Virginia Tech's cybersecurity club. Founded in 2011, the club is focused on nurturing student curiosity about cybersecurity in a variety of topics. 

How do I Join?

CyberVT is a diverse club open to individuals of all skill-sets and backgrounds. CyberVT meets every Thursday in New Classroom Building 170. Our primary means of communication is on Discord to which you can find an invite link via the button below.

Discord

What Does the Club Do?

In the past, we have done a variety of things. We regularly compete in CTFs such as MetaCTF and eCTF, give presentations on a plethora of security topics, etcetera. Our primary objective is to ensure each member is learning and more importantly having fun.
