We are a student organization in Computer Engineering and Computer Science at Virginia Tech founded in Spring 2011 with the goal of encouraging and expanding the study of cyber security and related fields. We aim to develop awareness and expertise in all areas of cyber security including physical, social, hardware, and software.

VTCSEC Summit

We at Virginia Tech host a Capture the Flag Competition each spring. This year, it will be held on March 28th! What makes our competition unique is we have a live network that competitors attack. This hands on experience provides a new twist to the normal connect to a box and hack it, you have to actually find some of your targets first! Get more information here!

Long-running CTF challenge
