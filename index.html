About CyberVT

We are a student-run cybersecurity organization that was founded in the spring of 2011 at Virginia Tech. Our goal is to encourage curiosity and learning about cybersecurity related topics for individuals of all skill levels. 

Interested in Joining?

CyberVT is a diverse club with members ranging from Computer Science and Computer Engineering to Business Information Technology. If you are interested in joining, find out more on our membership page.
