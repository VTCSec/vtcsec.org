Thank You Sponsors 

CyberVT is Grateful for Your Support

Why Sponsor?

CyberVT appreciates any and all contributions. Therefore, we have decided to incentivize sponsors supporting our club.

Recruit

Sponsors are allowed to recruit from Virginia Tech's most skilled group of hackers. Members will be encouraged to apply to sponsor companies.

Speak at Meetings

Sponsors may also come visit Virginia Tech and speak at CyberVT meetings.

Recognition

Sponsors will be given recognition for their generosity and a shout-out on our website and LinkedIn page.
